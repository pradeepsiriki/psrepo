Roles in a company ideally are from agile standpoint, Let's learn Jira also here

1. Business Analyst
    - Interacts with Customers, they work on BRD documents
2. Product manager
    - interacts with BA above and he will prioritizes the requirements (ex: I want this as mobile app, upi enabled, etc)
3. Product Owner
    - This man works on creating EPIC, feature and Jira so on
4. Software Artchitect
    - PO talks to solution architects, these guys will write High Level design and Low level design
5. Developers
    - these guys just break the ice and work on each piece towards completion
6. QA engineers
    -  help checkng the product quality and performance
7. DBA
    - help developers on the process , sestup , config via ci/cd
8. DevOps Engineers
    - Help in build, test, deploy automation to improve delivery efficiceny 
9. Release Managers (Optional)
    - plans and manages releases
10. SRE
    - Ensures uptime, performance and reliability post-release
11. Technical writers
    - Creates documentation for users and developers
